excerpt: "Computer vision project combining synthetic data augmentation, DETR-based detection, and SAM fine-tuning for muddy ballast imagery."
collection: portfolio
---

This project focused on improving ballast particle segmentation under muddy field conditions.

- Designed realistic synthetic materials for muddy ballast scenes.
- Trained a DETR-based detector and fine-tuned SAM on the augmented dataset.
- Improved segmentation performance by about 5% in both mAP and mAR.
- Validated the workflow on field data with 4.18% MAE in Fouling Index and 3.45% MAE in Particle Size Distribution.
