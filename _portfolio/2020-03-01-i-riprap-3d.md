I-RIPRAP 3D is an interactive software system for aggregate size and shape analysis.

- Built for physical-scale estimation and deep-learning-based 3D analysis.
- Supports measurement, visualization, and analysis of complex 3D data.
- Adopted by the Illinois Department of Transportation.
