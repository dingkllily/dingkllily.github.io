The Ballast Scanning Vehicle is an automated railway ballast scanning platform for large-scale field evaluation.

- Integrated area-scan and line-scan cameras, 3D scanning, and GPS positioning.
- Supported large-scale image and video processing pipelines in field environments.
- Connected to inspection software later adopted by the Federal Railroad Administration.
